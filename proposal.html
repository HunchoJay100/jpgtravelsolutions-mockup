04
The AI Agent

Your private research assistant
that never sleeps.

This is the game-changer. A custom AI agent built exclusively for JPG Travel Solutions, living directly inside Monday.com as a private app. Monday.com just launched their AI agent platform in March 2026, and the timing could not be better. This is not a generic chatbot. It is a purpose-built assistant trained on the luxury travel workflow.

Destination Research on Command

Ask the agent to research a destination, resort, or experience. It returns structured data, reviews, pricing context, and insider recommendations directly to the booking item in Monday.

Itinerary Draft Generation

Give it client preferences, dates, and a destination. It produces a polished first-draft itinerary that your advisors can refine and personalize, saving hours per booking.

Lead Categorization

New leads from the website get automatically categorized by travel type, budget tier, and urgency. The right advisor sees the right lead with the right context immediately.

Client Communication Drafts

Draft professional emails, follow-ups, and proposals in your brand voice. Your advisors review and send, keeping the human touch that luxury clients expect while saving significant drafting time.

Commission Tracking

Cross-reference expected commissions with received payments. Flag discrepancies automatically. No more money falling through the cracks because someone forgot to follow up with a supplier.

Smart Suggestions

Posts proactive suggestions to active bookings: restaurant recommendations, seasonal alerts, visa requirement changes, flight schedule updates. Your advisors always have the most current intelligence.

Important: this agent assists. It does not replace the human expertise and personal relationships that make luxury travel special. Specific permissions and guardrails ensure it stays in its lane while making your team dramatically more efficient.

"Technology should be invisible. Your clients should only feel the magic."

For Mrs. Paula

What this actually means
for your day-to-day.

We can talk about boards and automations and AI all day. But what matters is what changes when you sit down at your desk on a Monday morning. Here is what this transformation looks like in practice.

  • No more chasing payment deadlines manually. The system tracks every deposit, every final payment, and every confirmation. It notifies you and the client automatically.
  • No more building itineraries from scratch every single time. The AI agent drafts the first version based on client preferences, and your advisors refine and personalize it.
  • No more worrying about commission tracking falling through the cracks. Every expected payment is logged, tracked, and flagged if it does not arrive on time.
  • Automated client communication sequences handle the follow-ups, the pre-trip emails, the post-trip check-ins, and the birthday messages that keep relationships warm year-round.
  • The AI research agent handles the destination legwork. Instead of spending hours gathering information, your team reviews curated research and makes decisions faster.
  • Your clients get a branded app experience that makes every trip feel elevated from the moment they book to the moment they get home.
  • Your website finally reflects the quality of service you actually deliver, and it starts bringing in new clients organically rather than relying entirely on referrals.

You get to focus on what you love: the relationships, the creativity, the moments that turn a trip into something someone remembers for the rest of their life. The systems handle everything else.
